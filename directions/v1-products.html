build the routine

two minutes, morning. two minutes, night.

am

the morning.

  1. gentle cleanser.lukewarm water. 30 seconds. pat dry.
  2. hydrating toner.two pumps to damp skin. let it sink in.
  3. hydration serum.three drops. press in, don't rub.
  4. daily moisturizer.pearl-size. seal everything in.
  5. eye patches.optional. 10 minutes while the coffee brews.
pm

the night.

  1. gentle cleanser.double cleanse if you wore spf.
  2. hydrating toner.more generously than morning.
  3. hydration serum.three drops. let it work overnight.
  4. daily moisturizer.a slightly heavier pearl.
  5. spot patches.only if needed. they work while you sleep.
