held constant across all four.

brand name
iloveyouth — lowercase, one word, every surface, every direction.
tagline
youth starts with you. (exact casing, period included)
typeface
satoshi 300 / 400 / 500 — no heavier weights anywhere.
palette
shadow grey, frosted blue, ghost white, almond silk. four colors, locked.
accent rule
frosted blue is the hero. almond silk is supporting. ratio ≈ 10 : 1 per surface.
voice
ingredient-forward. plain language. every marketing number sourced.
periods
the period as a brand signature, scaled across each direction.
positioning
premium skincare. dtc-only. target retail: sephora.
