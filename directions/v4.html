01. · vitamin b3 · 5%

niacinamide.

brightens tone. minimises pore appearance. strengthens the skin barrier without irritation.

five percentfive percent is the upper end of well-tolerated niacinamide for daily use. above this, transient flushing increases with no measurable efficacy gain. below 2%, brightening signals fall off. 5% is the sweet spot of the range studied across the dermatology literature.

02. · lipid complex · 3 types

ceramides.

the same lipid molecules

your skin already producesceramides np, ap, and eop are endogenous lipids in the stratum corneum. when their ratio drops (age, stress, surfactant exposure), barrier function declines. supplementing with the same ratio rebuilds the barrier without needing occlusive ingredients.
. matched to skin's own ratio. no occlusion, no heaviness.
