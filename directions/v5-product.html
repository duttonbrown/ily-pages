the questions

good ones. answered honestly.

can i use this with retinol?.

yes. apply the serum to clean, dry skin first; let it absorb (60 seconds); then layer your retinol. hyaluronic acid + retinol is a well-studied pairing — the moisture buffers the retinoid's drying effect.

does this work for oily, acne-prone skin?.

yes. the formula is non-comedogenic and contains no acne-causing ingredients. niacinamide is one of the few actives proven to help acne-prone skin specifically — it calms inflammation and helps regulate sebum.

how long until i see results?.

most people feel the hydration boost the first time they use it. visible improvement in tone and texture takes 2–4 weeks of consistent use. our independent panel measured 96% reporting softer, more hydrated skin after one bottle.

is the bottle recyclable?.

yes. the bottle is pcr glass (recycled in your curbside bin). the dropper is plastic — pull it out before recycling and drop it at a #5 recycle point. we ship in 100% recycled paper, no plastic mailers.

what happens if it doesn't work?.

we'll refund the bottle, even if it's empty. 60-day satisfaction guarantee, no questions, no return shipping. we'd rather lose a sale than have you keep something that didn't work.
