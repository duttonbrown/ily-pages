the dehydrator

×drying alcohols

they evaporate fast and feel "fresh" — and leave skin drier than they found it. there's no version of this we'd ship.

the irritant

×witch hazel

it strips the barrier, especially on sensitive skin. there are gentler astringents. we don't need either.

the trigger

×fragrance

a top reactivity flag. nothing in our formula needs to smell like anything. it just has to work.

the bulk

×fillers

if it's not earning its place in the formula, it's not in the formula. ingredient transparency starts here.

the breakout cause

×comedogenic oils

if it clogs pores, it doesn't belong in a hydration product. acne-prone skin gets the same formula.

the legacy issue

×parabens / sulfates

we don't lead with "clean" as marketing. but if a category-standard ingredient irritates skin, we leave it out.
