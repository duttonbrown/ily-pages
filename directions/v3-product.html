what's in it

niacinamide (5%) — vitamin b3. brightens tone, minimises pore appearance, strengthens the barrier without irritation.

ceramides (np · ap · eop) — the same lipid molecules your skin already produces. they rebuild the barrier and lock moisture in.

hyaluronic acid — multi-weight. draws water deep into the skin, holds it there.

what's not in it

no drying alcohols. no witch hazel. no fragrance. no parabens. no sulfates. no comedogenic oils. nothing in here that undoes the good stuff.

how to use

after cleansing and toning, dispense 2–3 drops into clean palms. press into damp skin morning and evening. follow with moisturizer. layers under spf without pilling.
